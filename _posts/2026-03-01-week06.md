## How I want to contribute

I love open source. I am so looking forward to joining a group in this class and
working to better a project out there in the world. I don't really care what
kind of project it is as long as I feel like I have something to contribute.
I know that the nature of school group work and the fact that ultimately it is
not particularly consequential sometimes gets in the way of really committing
to a project, but I am confident that a good group in this class can push
something really hard. I am definitely also nervous about my contribution limit.
I work full time in addition to school and already engage in several
time-consuming endeavours outside of work and school. Luckily, I am feeling very
motivated to help do my part and help my teammates do theirs!

Regarding my current contributions, I have had time only to make small
contributions to wikipedia personally. I have been messaging the creator of
OpenXLIFF with small requested changes, but that's actually more related to work
than a pure interest in his project ;(.
