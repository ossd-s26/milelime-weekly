## Presentations

I loved seeing other groups' presentations. I missed the first day unfortunately
but the ones presented on 02/23 were wonderful. I loved seeing that people were
able to convert ideas into extensions built well and with all the limitations
expected of the short time frame.

The presentations were all rather uniform, we all sort of described and demoed
the extensions and gave basic explanations of how we contributed. The
presentations were not particularly stylized or variant. Contrasting that
with the presentations we watched this week, one can see that seasoned
presenters come with angles, choices, and preparation that exudes confidence
and knowledge.
